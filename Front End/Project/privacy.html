Privacy Policies

Your Career, Your Privacy: Our Commitment to Confidentiality in Job Consultation

  • We collect personal information solely for the purpose of providing job consultation services. This information includes your resume, contact details, and preferences, which we use to match you with suitable job opportunities.
  • Your personal information is treated with the utmost confidentiality. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • It's important to keep your profile information accurate and up to date. This ensures that we can provide you with the best job opportunities tailored to your skills and preferences.
  • We may contact you via email or phone to discuss potential job opportunities, provide updates, and offer career advice. You can opt out of communication at any time.
  • We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our systems are regularly monitored and updated to ensure data security.
  • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather non-personal information about site usage. You can adjust your browser settings to control cookies or opt out of tracking.
  • When you apply for a job through our platform, your application materials are shared with potential employers. We ensure that your data is only shared with relevant parties in the hiring process.
  • We may use third-party services to assist in providing our services, such as email providers and analytics tools. These providers are carefully selected and are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored on our platform. You can update your preferences or delete your account at any time.
